About MicroAdventures

MicroAdventures is an iOS travel app designed for curious explorers. It connects travelers with local events, meetups, and spontaneous experiences, powered by the Meetup API. Whether you're exploring a new city or looking for hidden gems nearby, MicroAdventures makes it easy to find events that match your interests.

Meetup Integration

OAuth Disclosure: This app uses the Meetup API to show relevant events nearby. You may be asked to log in with your Meetup account to enable this feature. Your account information will only be used to display events and will not be shared without your consent.
